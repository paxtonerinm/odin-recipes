Vegan Lasagna

Description

This delicious vegan lasagna recipe almost perfectly emulates the real thing, making it the perfect meal for almost any occasion. Two types of tofu, with the help of some nutritional yeast and spices, comprise the "ricotta" in the dish, allowing for the classic texture and cheesy taste you find in real lasagna! Thanks to Forks Over Knives for providing this recipe.

Taste isn't the only noteworthy aspect of this dish, it's also good for you! The spinach, tofu, and no-salt-added sauce provide essential nutrients without compromising on taste.

Ingredients

  • 1 (12.3-ounce) package silken tofu
  • 1 pound fresh water-packed firm tofu
  • 2 teaspoons minced garlic
  • ¼ cup nutritional yeast
  • ½ teaspoon sea salt
  • ½ teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon parsley flakes
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • ¼ cup fresh lemon juice
  • ¼ cup soy milk
  • 1−2 pounds fresh spinach, lightly steamed
  • 8 ounces no-boil lasagna noodles
  • 7 cups fat-free pasta sauce
  • ¼ cup soy parmesan or Parma vegan parmesan
